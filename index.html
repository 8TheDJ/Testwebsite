The Quadratic Formula

What is the Quadratic Formula?

The quadratic formula is used to find the solutions (roots) of a quadratic equation of the form:

ax² + bx + c = 0

The Formula

To solve for x, the formula is:

x = (-b ± √(b² - 4ac)) / 2a

Visualizing the Formula

This formula allows us to find the values of x that satisfy the equation. By plugging in the values of a, b, and c, we can compute the roots.
